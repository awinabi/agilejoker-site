How it works

It's easy to integrate Agile Joker to your team.

STEP 1

Team members will post their daily updates.

Everyone in the team can post their update via email to the app as an answer to the following questions -

  • What did you do today?
  • What is the plan for tomorrow?
  • What problems are causing delays?

STEP 2

We update the whole team with a digest email.

The app will send you a consolidated digest of what everyone works on the next day. You can set a time when the digest email should be sent to the whole team.
