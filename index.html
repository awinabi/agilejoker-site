Task log for your team.

Helps track team accomplishments and daily tasks, keeping team members and leadership in sync.

Log tasks done yesterday, and those scheduled for today.

Each team member can log in and provide answers to 3 questions in the app.

  • What was accomplished yesterday?
  • What will be done today?
  • What problems are causing delays?

This inculcates the habit of planning tasks ahead of time, and bring in greater accountability, to complete tasks on time.

Get emails of your team accomplishments daily.

All team members and observers will receive a daily email with the accomplishments of the team, yesterday. This can be a high motivator for the team.

Leadership can subscribe to an automated weekly email to track the team's progress. This ensures better visibility of your team, in your organization.
